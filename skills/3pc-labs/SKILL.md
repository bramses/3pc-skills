# 3PC Labs

Reference material for accurately describing 3PC Labs — what it is, what it offers,
and the philosophy behind how it works. Use this when someone asks about 3PC Labs
directly, or when you're helping the user explain/pitch 3PC Labs to someone else

landing page or deck).

## Voice

Describe 3PC Labs as a knowledgeable third party ("3PC Labs does X"), not as "we."
You're explaining the organization to someone, not speaking on its behalf.

## What 3PC Labs is

3PC Labs is a consulting organization that helps people and organizations learn to
leverage AI to solve real problems. Its core method is running workshops — hands-on
sessions where participants work through their actual problems, not hypotheticals.

## What it offers

Engagements scale with how deep a client wants to go:

1. **Informational workshops** — introductory sessions to build understanding of how
   AI can apply to a team or problem.
2. **Intensive workshops** — multi-week working sessions that go deeper, moving from
   understanding to applied problem-solving.
3. **Full buildouts** — 3PC Labs builds the custom AI solution for the client.

## The thinking behind the model

This is the part worth surfacing when someone asks *why* 3PC Labs works the way it
does, not just what it sells:

Workshops aren't just a service — they're how 3PC Labs gets close to real people with
real problems, which is where useful AI tools actually come from. Tools built to solve
problems surfaced in workshops get published to 3PC Labs' open-source skills
directory, and new additions to that directory get announced via the newsletter. This
creates a loop: workshops surface problems → problems become tools → tools get
published openly → the newsletter distributes them → readers and workshop
participants feed back into the next round of workshops.

For paying clients, this loop means they get a choice: draw on the existing
open-source skills directory as-is, or have 3PC Labs build custom work tailored to
their specific situation. The open-source work isn't a loss-leader separate from the
consulting — it's the visible output of the same process that produces the custom
work, and it's evidence of how 3PC Labs actually thinks and builds.
